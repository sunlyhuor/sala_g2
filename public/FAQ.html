How does online learning work at Sala?

At Sala, online learning is comprised of two components:

1. High quality recorded lectures by certified instructors
2. Virtual assessments & assignments

As we follow a Facilitated Inverted Classroom Model (FICM) , Sala promotes student achievement and success, through self-directed learning. While our remote learning is comprehensive and flexible, it is personalized and tailored to support students on a 1v1 basis.

Is online education as effective as face-to-face instruction?

Online education may seem relatively new, but years of research suggests it can be just as effective as traditional coursework, and often more so. According to a U.S. Department of Education analysis of more than 1,000 learning studies, online students tend to outperform classroom-based students across most disciplines and demographics.
